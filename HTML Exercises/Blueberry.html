Ingredients

Topping:

  • 1-1/2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon cold water

Crust:

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumb (about 16 squares)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter (melted)

Filling:

  • 3 8 ounce packages cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla exract
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature (lightly beaten)

Instructions

Topping:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°. In a small saucepan, combine the blueberries, sugar and lemon juice.
  2. Cook and stir over low heat for 5 minutes or until the berries are softened.
  3. Combine cornstarch and water until smooth; stir into the blueberry mixture.
  4. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
  5. Remove from the heat; cool to room temperature.
  6. Transfer to a blender; cover and process until smooth. If desired, strain out solids.

Crust:

  1. For crust, in a small bowl, combine the crumbs and sugar; stir in the butter.
  2. Press onto the bottom of a greased 9-in. springform pan.
  3. Place pan on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10 minutes.
  5. Cool on a wire rack.

Filling:

  1. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  2. Beat in the sour cream, flour and vanilla.
  3. Add eggs; beat on low speed just until combined.
  4. Pour filling over crust.
  5. Drizzle with 4 tablespoons blueberry mixture; cut through batter with a knife to swirl.

Final Baking Instructions:

  1. Return pan to baking sheet. Bake until center is almost set, 40-45 minutes.
  2. Cool on a wire rack for 1 hour; cover and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Serve with remaining blueberry sauce.
  4. Refrigerate leftovers.
