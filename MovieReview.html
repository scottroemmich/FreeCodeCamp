Movie Review: Top Gun: Maverick (2022)

Top Gun: Maverick was one of the best Top Gun movies I've seen! (still yet to see Top Gun (2025). This movie kept me on the edge of my seat. I was fixated on the scren all two hourse and ten minutes of this Action Packed adventure. What a ride. I highly recommend this video to anyone seeking an adventure. You won't be dissapointed.
